About Me

As an owner of Florida Indian River Groves, the leading provider of groves fresh fruit for school fundraisers, Jeff Knowles has assisted thousands of schools in raising millions of dollars for travel and general operating expenses. Jeff manages the day-to-day operations of the company and is actively involved in the company's new, ecommerce initiatives.
A graduate of the College of Business at the University of Central Florida, Jeff went on to earn an MBA degreee, as part of the innaugral class of the Rollins College Key Exectuive MBA program.
Jeff devotes his time to numerous community causes, including SafeSpace, Give Kids the World, and the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society.
